Love of Scottish History

and my (distant) family connection

Seeing "Braveheart" (1995) sent me home moved by Scotland's fight to gain their freedom from their English overlords. And curious about the real life of William Wallace and his comrades-in-arms, who routed the English at Stirling in the ninth month of 1297. I referenced my Scottish Encyclopedia to check the validity of the facts. True.

Though I had been interested in the history of Scotland, this stirred my emotions to find out more about my own history. Scottish history.
